WHO EVEN IS THIS GUY?

Hello there! I am BENJAMIN PANG, a primarily self-taught programmer and artist who is currently interested in the bridging of art and technology to create new modes of expression.

I was born and raised in California's Bay Area and currently reside in Abu Dhabi, UAE for undergraduate studies at NYU Abu Dhabi. First exposed to coding in middle school, I now have + years of practical programming experience. This experience is supplemented by academic as well as practical training in a variety of skills related to design, art, and engineering. I hope to apply these skills to create meaningful works that address the many issues of our technology-saturated world.
